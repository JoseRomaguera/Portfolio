Coding Day

What is Coding Day?

In Coding Day, you are a videogame developer whose project is about to be released, but a sinister entity threatens its publication, and you must survive it throughout the night in a dark office building full of mysteries and dangers.

The project was initiated in 2022 by Dreamlight Games and is still under development. It is being created using Unity and C#, following the Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) paradigm. The team consists of one artist, a designer, and myself as the programmer.

My Contribution

I was part of the project from December 2022 until June 2024.

  • UI programmer.
